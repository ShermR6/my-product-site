Setup

Four steps to <5s alerts

01
Position Antenna
Mount the stubby antenna at height — roof or window with clear sky view. Higher = more range.
02
Connect Hardware
Plug the Pro Stick Plus into your Pi's USB port. Connect power and ethernet or WiFi.
03
Run the Script
Start finalping_ground.py with your API key and airport ICAO. dump1090 starts automatically.
04
Enable in App
Toggle "Ground Station" on in the FinalPing desktop app. First local ping appears within seconds.
