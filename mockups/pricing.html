Questions & answers

What is a license key? ↓
FinalPing uses a one-time license key — no login required to use the app. Purchase a key, paste it into the desktop app, and alerts start immediately. Keys are emailed to you after purchase.
Can I track aircraft I don't own? ↓
Yes. You can track any aircraft by its ICAO hex or tail number, whether it's yours or not. FBO operators commonly use FinalPing to monitor all inbound traffic.
What is a Ground Station? ↓
A local ADS-B receiver (RTL-SDR dongle + Raspberry Pi + dump1090) that FinalPing can connect to directly. You get sub-5-second local alerts that don't depend on the cloud data pipeline.
Do I need to keep the app open? ↓
Yes, the desktop app needs to be running. It minimizes to your system tray and uses minimal resources. The auto-launch on login option keeps it running in the background automatically.
