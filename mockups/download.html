Installation

1
Download the installer
Run FinalPingSetup.exe. Windows may show a SmartScreen prompt — click "More info" then "Run anyway." The app is unsigned for now.
2
Launch & activate
Open FinalPing and enter your license key. Keys are delivered by email after purchasing a plan. Free trial starts immediately without a key.
3
Configure & go
Set your home airport ICAO, add tail numbers, connect your notification channels. Alerts start within seconds of your first aircraft ping.
